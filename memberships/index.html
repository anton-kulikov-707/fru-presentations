FundraiseUp's Role

What is Memberships?

Definition

  • Donation model where payment confers supporter status
  • Structure & lifecycle governed by Nonprofit’s CRM
FundraiseUp's Role: Execution
Process payments, explicit semantics, generate receipts, reflect CRM status.

What we do NOT do

  • Define tiers or benefits
  • Manage renewal logic
  • Own lifecycle rules

"CRM Primacy: execution without lifecycle ownership"

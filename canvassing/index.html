Canvassing via Vendors

Covering the Offline Donation Channel

What canvassing is

Offline, in-person donor acquisition

  • Face-to-face fundraising: Street Canvassing, Private Site Canvassing (malls, events), Door-to-Door
  • Professional canvassers, high volume
  • Focus on recurring donations
  • Proven, non-digital channel

A new donation channel, but outsourced surface

Why orgs already use vendors

Useful partners

Vendors own:

  • Workforce & training
  • Field ops & compliance
  • Devices & logistics
  • Payment processing

Benefits:

  • Predictable acquisition at scale
  • Clear ROI model: Org hire Vendor, Vendor acquires donors, Org gets recurring donations

Why this won’t move to Tap-to-Pay

  • Tap-to-Pay = org’s who can run F2F, D2D, events canvassing themselves
  • Canvassing via Vendors = outsourced acquisition
  • Different OPS, different economics

No competition. No overlap.

# Canvassing via Vendors  
### Covering the Offline Donation Channel

---

## 1. What canvassing is  
**Offline, in-person donor acquisition**

- Face-to-face fundraising: Street Canvassing, Private Site Canvassing in malls or at events, Door-to-Door
- Professional canvassers, high volume  
- Recurring-first donations focus 
- Proven, non-digital channel  

> Not a product surface. A donation channel.

---

## 2. Why orgs already use vendors  
**Structural reasons**

- Vendors own:
  - Workforce & training  
  - Field ops & compliance  
  - Devices & logistics  
- Predictable acquisition at scale  
- Clear ROI model: Org hire Vendor, Vendor acquires donors, Org gets recurring donations

**Why this won’t move to Tap-to-Pay**
- Tap-to-Pay = org’s who can run F2F, D2D, events canvassing themselves 
- Canvassing via Vendors = outsourced acquisition  
- Different economics, different jobs  

> No competition. No overlap.

---

## 3. How we partner  
**Clear separation of responsibilities**

- Vendor:
  - Frontend & field experience  
  - Canvasser workflows  
- FundraiseUp:
  - Payment processing  
  - Subscriptions & lifecycle  
  - Donor stewardship  
- Organization:
  - Owns the donor relationship  

> Vendor FE. Our BE. Org owns donors.

---

## 4. Donor data flow  
**Simple, consistent, familiar**

- FundraiseUp = system of record  
- Donor data captured once  
- Standard confirmations & exports  
- Same donor model as online donations  

> Offline acquisition, same backend logic.
